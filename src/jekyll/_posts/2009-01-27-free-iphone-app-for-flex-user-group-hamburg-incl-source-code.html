[UPDATE 02/11/09] flexughh app is available on iTunes store! All links has been updated. Get it for free ;)

It's done! My first iPhone app is uploaded to Apple's app store. It's an app for the Flex UG in Hamburg / Germany, called "flexughh". Because I'm very excited about all the enthusiastic flexughh-members I decided to offer this app for free including its full source code written in Objective-C as open source! Today I will present the new flexughh app at our flexughh-Meeting. If you don't have the chance to attend it, here are some screen shots.
