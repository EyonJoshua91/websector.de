In May 2006 I designed and programmed a Flash game called "WS-Parachute", which is a clone of iPods "Parachute". Now "WS-Parachute" has its own community worldwide. The most faithful fans are from Japan ;-). Hey guys, thanks for visiting!

Screen shot

Details

  • It's written in object-oriented ActionScript 2.
  • It's compatible with MTASC.
  • The high score based on AMFPHP.
  • Free download: You'll find the source of its game engine on its website.
